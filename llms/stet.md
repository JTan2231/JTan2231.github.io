# Stet

- Source: https://stet.ink
- Retrieved: 2026-01-24

## Summary
Stet is a tool for sharpening your writing and clarifying your thinking.

## Observed UI copy (from the client app bundle)
- Setup New Draft -- work with Stet to outline your Audience and Goals.
- Blank Page -- start writing right away.
- Studio / Settings tabs.
- Onboarding prompts: "Who are you writing for?" and "What is the goal of this essay?"

## Notes
- The site is a client-rendered app; the initial HTML contains only the root container and loads content via JavaScript.
